🌞 Summer Survival Guide — May North India
Peak heat 40–44°C. These tips are MANDATORY for family comfort + grandma safety.
⏰Heat Window Rule — Outdoor activities ONLY 6–10 AM & 5–8 PM. Midday 11 AM–4 PM = AC hotel rest. Non-negotiable for elderly.
💧Hydration — 3–4 litres water/person/day MINIMUM. Carry ORS/Electral sachets. Give grandma ORS every morning before step-out.
👕Clothing — Light full-sleeve cotton (blocks sun, prevents overheating). Carry small towel + cooling spray/wet cloth. Avoid synthetics.
💊Medical Kit — ORS sachets, Paracetamol, Digene (acidity), Electral powder, Band-aids, grandma's regular meds + 2-day extra supply. Carry doctor's letter listing medications.
🗣️Language — Hindi primary. Download Google Translate offline (Hindi ↔ Tamil ↔ English). Carry handwritten Hindi note with hotel address for autos.
🪪Aadhaar — Carry ORIGINAL Aadhaar all 5 members. Required: Kashi Vishwanath, Ram Mandir, some hotels. Keep photocopies too.
♿Wheelchair / Walking Aid — If grandma doesn't have, rent from hotel in Varanasi. Folding stick recommended for all elderly throughout trip.
👗Temple Dress Code — No shorts, no sleeveless inside temples. Sisters carry stole/dupatta. Men avoid sleeveless vests. Shoes come off.
📱Photography — Phones NOT allowed inside Ram Mandir / Kashi Vishwanath. Use free locker (₹20). Take photos outside gates only.
🍛Food Comfort — South Indian available ALL cities: Saravana Bhavan (Delhi, Agra), Udupi Bhavan (Varanasi). No food anxiety. Most restaurants serve dosa/idli breakfast.
